Mona Lisa

(1503-1506)

La Gioconda

The Mona Lisa is a half-length portrait painting by the Italian artist
Leonardo da Vinci. It is considered an archetypal masterpiece of the Italian
Renaissance, and has been described as "the best known, the most visited,
the most written about, the most sung about, the most parodied work of art
in the world".


The painting is likely of the Italian noblewoman Lisa Gherardini
,[9] the wife of Francesco del Giocondo, and is in oil on a white Lombardy poplar panel.
It had been believed to have been painted between 1503 and 1506
