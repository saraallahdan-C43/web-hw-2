the night watch

(1642)

De Nachtwacht

The Night Watch , is a 1642 painting by Rembrandt van Rijn. It is
in the collection of the Amsterdam Museum but is prominently displayed in the
Rijksmuseum as the best-known painting in its collection. The Night Watch is one
of the most famous Dutch Golden Age paintings.


The painting is famous for three things: its colossal size (363 cm × 437 cm (11.91 ft × 14.34 ft)),
the dramatic use of light and shadow (tenebrism) and the perception of motion
in what would have traditionally been a static military group portrait.
